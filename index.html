Welcome to The Surgical Armamentarium

A two day course designed to help those in early stages of their surgical cateer acquire the foundations of knowledge and skills essential for safe practice. It covers the suubjects that are rarely taught in the surgical curriculum. From how to consent to laparoscopic suturing...and everything in-between!

  • Tutorials, videos and demonstrations
  • Wet specimens
  • Laparoscopic simulators
  • 14 CPD Points (ALSGBI)
